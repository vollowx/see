Accessibility, 无障碍功能 If you do not set tag attribute to custom inner element, when somebody asks accessibility utilities, "Hey bro, what's that texts?", then accessibility utilities will answer, "Oh, that is just a StaticText". But in fact, it is a heading (h1) 如果你不用 tag 属性来自定义内部元素, 当有人问无障碍工具时: "嘿! 那段文字是什么?", 无障碍工具会这样回答: "噢! 那只是一段 StaticText (静态文字) 罢了". 但事实上, 那是一段 heading (标题, h1)

Attributes, 属性 Types:
  • single choice means choose one of all names
  • toggle means give a attribute with no content for true, do not give this attribute for false
